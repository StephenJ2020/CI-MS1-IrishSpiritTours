Dublin Distillery / Brewery Tours

Teeling Whiskey Distillery

All of our tours are fully guided and offer visitors the chance to experience the complete whiskey making process with one of our Teeling Whiskey Ambassadors. Each of our tours conclude with a tasting of our award winning Teeling Whiskeys, with a selection of tasting options available to suit the novice and aficionado alike.

A selection of pre-book language tour options also available. The Teeling Whiskey Distillery is open 7 days a week, with tours running every 20 mins from 10:00am to 5:40pm. Due to the high volume of visitors to the distillery at present please do book well in advance in order to secure your booking.

Ticket prices start from 17.00 per person

Rascals Brewing

Rascals Brewing was founded in 2014 by Emma Devlin and Cathal O’Donoghue. The pair were living and working in New Zealand and developed a taste and passion for the booming Kiwi craft beer industry. In the spirit of adventure, they decided to change careers entirely and set up their own craft brewery back home in Dublin. Since then, Rascals has brewed all manner of wonderful beer, from Chardonnay Saisons to Mint Choc Stouts to Strawberry Vanilla Milkshake IPAs.

Ticket prices start from 20.00 per person

Pearse Lyons Distillery

Pearse Lyons Distillery at St. James has been born out of a personal passion for brewing and distilling, an entrepreneurial spirit and a deep rooted family connection with The Liberties. “We have always dreamed about having an Irish Whiskey distillery in Dublin. We set upon a journey of finding the perfect location, and in 2013, we discovered St. James’ Church and were drawn to its rich history and The Liberties.

The location in the centre of Dublin’s historic whiskey district was ideal, and the cast of colourful characters associated with St.James’ Church, including my grandfather, John Hubert Lyons who is buried in the graveyard, made it even more special.
(Pearse Lyons, the late Founder of Pearse Lyons Distillery)

Ticket prices start from 18.00 per person

Other Festivals:

Bloom Garden Show

Each year Bloom attracts over 110,000 to the 70 acre site at the Visitor's Centre in the Phoenix Park, Dublin.

With something for everyone and fun for all the family, visitors to Bloom will enjoy cookery and craft demonstrations, free gardening advice from the experts, Irish food produce, live entertainment and gardening workshops.

June Bank Holiday Weekend

Ticket prices start from 16.00 per person

Dublin Horse Show

Since it was first held in 1864 the Horse Show has become a Dublin institution. A celebration of Ireland's affinity with the horse, from the best show horses to the best international show jumpers.

It is one of Ireland's largest events, a highlight of the summer, each year welcoming tens of thousands of people from Ireland and all over the world.

18-22 August 2021

Ticket prices start from 22.00 per person

The Big Grill Festival

The festival celebrates the art of cooking with fire & smoke bringing chefs cooks and pitmasters from Ireland, UK and further along with over 20,000 visitors to smell, taste, discover and learn about all kinds of barbecuing, grilling, smoking, vegetables, meats, delicacies and drinks.

Over 20 restaurants join for the weekend, with the only rule being they must cook with live fire using natural charcoal and wood only; no gas or electricity!

AUGUST 12th – 15th 2021

Ticket prices start from 22.50 per person
