A Cultural Tour of Ireland's Ancient East

This 7 night tour features some of the finest local food producers, craft breweries and distilleries that Ireland has to offer!

Tour Itinerary

Day One: Walking Food Tour of Dublin & Private Whiskey Tasting

Today is really an arrival day to allow everyone to arrive in their own time as we understand that some of you will be travelling in from further afield than others. However for those of you who are able to arrive a little earlier in the day we do have some fun activities planned to introduce to your fellow tour companions while getting a taste of what our capitol city has to offer.

Read more

We'll meet in the hotel bar at 2pm and enjoy a complimentary glass of prosecco before heading out for a 3 hour walking food tour of some of Dublin's hidden gems and of course a few old reliables. We should be back at the hotel by 5:30pm and while we doubt you'll be looking for a big dinner have the delicious treats you will have sampled on the food tour, we will give you a few hours to rest and relax. We'll re-group in the hotel reception area at 8pm and take a short transfer over to the Church Bar were we have an area reserved for a private Whiskey Tasting hosted by Glen from Distilled Ireland.

The Whiskey Tasting event lasts approx. an hour and half, so at 10pm your tour leader will arrange the transfer back to the hotel, although if some of you wish to sample a little more of Dublin's lively Saturday night nightlife then you can of cousre make your own way back to the hotel a little later.
